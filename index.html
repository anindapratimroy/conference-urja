About the Workshop

Overview

Featuring expert lectures and hands-on tutorials, this workshop aims to build expertise in GEANT4 applications for high-energy physics, space astrophysics, and particle physics, while strengthening the GEANT4 community in India.

This interdisciplinary workshop is targeted at graduate students (MSc and PhD), early career researchers, and scientific staff working in high-energy physics and/or X-ray/gamma-ray astronomy. Participants will build and run their own GEANT4 models and apply them to their own research.

The main aim is capacity building towards experimental projects in high energy physics, space astrophysics, and particle physics, while gathering a thriving community of GEANT4 users across India's premier research institutions.
